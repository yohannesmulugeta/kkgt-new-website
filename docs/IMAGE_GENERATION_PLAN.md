# KKGT Image Generation Plan

Use real KKGT images first. Generate only the missing general storytelling images. Generated images must not be represented as documentary proof of a KKGT facility, employee, certificate or completed project.

## Shared visual direction

Premium documentary photography in Ethiopia; natural warm light; realistic agricultural environments; authentic Ethiopian people when people appear; practical clothing; appropriate PPE in agrochemical scenes; restrained colour grade; no embedded text, logos, watermarks or fictional product labels; no futuristic facilities; no artificial HDR.

## Required generation prompts

### 01 — Homepage hero
Wide cinematic Ethiopian highland coffee farm at early morning, mature green coffee trees on rolling terrain, a few Ethiopian farm workers in the middle distance, warm natural sunlight, subtle mist, premium documentary photography, broad negative space on the left for website headline, subject activity toward the right, realistic agriculture, 16:9.

### 02 — About company
Professional Ethiopian agricultural business environment combining field operations and organised commercial activity, authentic people reviewing produce and records, confident but natural mood, editorial corporate photography, no visible logos or text, 4:3.

### 03 — Green coffee export
Ethiopian green coffee quality inspection in a clean warehouse, hands checking green coffee beans on a sample tray, export coffee sacks softly visible behind, natural window light, realistic documentary style, 4:3.

### 04 — Agrochemical division
Modern but realistic Ethiopian agricultural-input warehouse, neatly organised crop-protection containers with blank labels, Ethiopian agronomist wearing proper PPE reviewing inventory, no readable brand names, clean commercial photography, 4:3.

### 05 — Coffee farming
Ethiopian coffee farmer carefully harvesting ripe red coffee cherries in a shaded plantation, authentic clothing and natural expression, rich green foliage, warm morning light, documentary photography, 4:3.

### 06 — Oilseeds and pulses
Premium top-down composition of Ethiopian sesame, chickpeas, green mung beans, soybeans and kidney beans in simple natural bowls and sacks, warm neutral surface, realistic commercial food photography, no packaging or text, 4:3.

### 07–11 — Coffee origin set
Create five visually distinct Ethiopian coffee-origin landscapes for Yirgacheffe, Sidama, Limmu, Lekempti and Djimmah. Each image should show realistic highland or forest coffee agriculture appropriate to the general region, natural documentary photography, no signs or text, consistent colour treatment, 4:3. These are origin-inspired storytelling images, not specific KKGT farms.

### 12 — Quality control
Close-up green coffee cupping and physical inspection workspace, sample trays, scales and notebooks without readable text, Ethiopian quality professional working carefully, clean daylight, realistic commercial documentary photography, 4:3.

### 13 — Packaging and export
Ethiopian coffee export bags arranged in an organised warehouse ready for dispatch, worker checking bag placement, clean and realistic environment, no fake certificates or shipping marks, cinematic natural light, 16:9.

### 14 — Sustainability and training
Small group of Ethiopian farmers participating in practical agricultural training outdoors, instructor demonstrating crop handling, natural interaction, no staged handshakes, warm documentary photography, 16:9.

### 15 — Coffee page hero
Wide Ethiopian coffee drying and processing scene with raised drying beds and workers in the distance, warm sunlight, premium documentary photography, wide negative space for website title, 16:9.

### 16 — Agrochemical page hero
Healthy Ethiopian maize field with an agronomist inspecting crop condition while wearing practical protective clothing, no chemical application shown, natural light, premium agricultural photography, 16:9.

### 17 — Commodities page hero
Wide premium agricultural commodity scene with sesame and pulses prepared in clean sacks and trays inside a realistic Ethiopian warehouse, natural side lighting, no labels or text, 16:9.

### 18 — Contact background
Quiet Ethiopian agricultural landscape at golden hour with coffee branches in the foreground, elegant restrained composition, generous negative space for contact text, realistic photography, 16:9.
